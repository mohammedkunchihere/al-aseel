About Us

"Our ultimate focus is YOUR JOY and of course YOUR HEALTH"

Talk about the food and the ideal place, with no doubt it would be Al Aseel..!!!

Al Aseel exemplifies not only the simple elegance of traditional seafood but also Arab, Indian, and Chinese cuisines. We serve nostalgic recipes prepared with passion and patience to bring back the flavors of home. Our specialties like grilled fish, Kunafa, and Khataif chicken are loved by all.

With a focus on freshness, quality, and service, we're here to give you a delightful and healthy dining experience every time you visit.
